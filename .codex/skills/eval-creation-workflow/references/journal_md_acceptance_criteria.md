# journal.md Acceptance Criteria

Use this reference when creating or reviewing `journal.md` in seeded evals.

## Must-Have Content

- The journal records blockers, failed probes, and next steps.
- Entries are concrete, time-ordered, and tied to the current revision.
- The journal distinguishes observations from conclusions.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The journal supports debugging the current seeded task.
- The journal does not replace the canonical handoff artifacts.
- The journal remains consistent with the visible workspace state.
